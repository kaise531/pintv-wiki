Anne

Personality

Anne's handwriting is distinctly different from Kogan's.

History

The mysterious previous owner of Kogan's journal. They have been investigating the Pandora Institute and bought the facility two years ago on January 5th, 2021.

The mantle of property manager has now been passed on to Kogan along with possession of the Journal. They state that by the time Kogan reads their October 1st message, they will already be gone.
