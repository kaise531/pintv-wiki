February 2024

Client

Programs Used

Over the course of a 7.5-week class, I was tasked to design and iterate on an informational poster or infographic. This was done in five stages according to the course requirements: Written Proposal (see in full report below), Copywriting and Typography, Design, Layout, and Refinement (pictured above). I utilized critique from both my professor and my peers to continue to elevate and experiment with this piece. I improved my understanding of visual hierarchy, strengthened my eye for layout design, and struck a pleasant balance between graphical elements and white space.

Port of Mars Social Media Campaign

Project Date

March 2024

Client

GIT 402

Programs Used

Photoshop

For this project, I was tasked to create a social media campaign proposal (see link below) for the biannual ASU Port of Mars competition. Along with the written proposal, I created three graphics with an original call to action that would be posted to Instagram, X, and TikTok.

In Photoshop, I started with a real image of the surface of Mars, and added my call to action text. I created a layer style on the text that would make it appear to be part of the landscape with a chiseled effect. The outer glow both plays into the sci-fi theming of the tournament while also making the brown text stand out from the brown background. The same style was then applied to both the Port of Mars and ASU logos for cohesion. This text was formatted to fit each of the three aspect ratios. I then composited in a photo of a Mars base camp simulator facility into varying spots in each post, being careful to blend the facility perfectly into its surroundings. The X image had more room to play with, so I also added the five different character types you can play as into the image. Once I was done with all three posts, I created mockups of what they would look like published to their respective platforms, with captions to match.

Through this project, I demonstrated my proficiency in several areas: compositing, calls to action, project management, and social media marketing.
