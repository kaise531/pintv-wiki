Oliver
 Security Guard

Personality

Friendly and personable. He can appear unassuming, but he has the skills and strength for his job. He is very perceptive and can tell when something is off with Kogan. Videography is a hobby of his. Claustrophobic.

History

Oliver was once a military medic, but left the profession. He is new to the Pandora Apartments and remembers meeting Kogan on his first day a couple of weeks before September 29th.

Facts & Important Information
  • Anne indicates him as suspicious.
  • Ordered security cameras for Kogan.
  • He has brought up the events of September 29th to his coworkers as a kind of joke.
