Kogan Mendez

Personality

Kogan is standoffish and jumpy in his current state. He is unwilling to build any relationships with tenants or security staff. He has his own problems to deal with. He is the current property manager of the Pandora Apartments.

History

Kogan's mother was institutionalized at the Pandora Institute when he was young due to schizophrenia. When she died, Kogan came to collect her belongings from the facility as well as a storage unit, leaving everything untouched except for a handmade vase. In times when Kogan was struggling, the vase acted as a source of comfort.

No mention is made of Kogan's father. He has been to college and owned an apartment before his latest memory lapse, but it has not been revealed what his occupation was.

Kogan has a history of memory lapses and hallucinations, which has impeded his ability to form relationships. People around him believed he may have inherited his mother's schizophrenia.

Facts & Important Information
  • All of his mother's belongings from the storage unit are in his apartment, Unit 104.
  • Kogan estimates that this latest memory lapse was a duration of 1-2 years.
  • Seems to be tied to someone named Anne through the Journal. Mrs. Greene thought his name was Anne for a month.
